FAQs

Q: How do I rent a dress?

A: Browse our shop, add your favorite dress to the cart, select the number of rental days (1-5), and proceed to checkout to complete your payment.

Q: What is the rental period?

A: You can rent a dress for 1 to 5 days. The total cost is calculated based on the number of days you select during checkout.

Q: How do I return a dress?

A: Return the dress within 2 days of the rental end date using the provided return label. Visit our Returns page for more details.

Q: What if the dress doesn’t fit?

A: Refer to our Size Guide or contact us at support@fancydress.com for assistance. Exchanges may be available depending on stock.

Q: Can I cancel my order?

A: Yes, cancellations are allowed within 24 hours of placing the order. Please contact us at support@fancydress.com to cancel.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: We accept payments via Razorpay, including credit/debit cards and UPI. Visit our Checkout page for more information.
