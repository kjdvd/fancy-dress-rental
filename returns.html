Returns

At FancyDress, we want to ensure your rental experience is hassle-free. Please review our return policy below:

  • Dresses must be returned within 2 days of the rental end date.
  • Items should be returned in their original condition, clean and undamaged.
  • Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective.
  • To initiate a return, contact us at support@fancydress.com with your order number.

For any issues, our customer service team is here to help. Reach out via phone at +1-800-555-1234 or email.
