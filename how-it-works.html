How It Works

Step 1: Browse and Select

Explore our collection of dresses, filter by style or occasion, and add your favorites to the cart.

Step 2: Choose Rental Period

Select the number of rental days (1-5 days) that suit your needs during checkout.

Step 3: Pay and Receive

Complete the payment online, and we’ll ship the dress to your address. Track your order status via email.

Step 4: Return

Return the dress within 2 days of the rental end date using our provided return label. Check our Returns page for details.
